About the Firm

Protecting Your Rights & Interests in Uzbekistan

Founded in 2022, All-Legal specializes in safeguarding the legal rights of both corporate entities and individuals within the Republic of Uzbekistan. We are dedicated to providing clear, actionable counsel in a rapidly evolving legal landscape.

Our firm serves as a vital bridge for foreign investment, offering comprehensive support in corporate law, tax planning, contract negotiation, and migration issues. We expertly manage the complexities of license acquisition, visas, and work permits, ensuring smooth operations for international clients.

Our team of 7 qualified lawyers brings deep expertise to every case. We are proud of our successful history assisting over 70 legal entities across key sectors including construction, tourism, trade, IT, and finance.
